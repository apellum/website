Iowa Cubs

Triple A

The Iowa Cubs are the highest MiLB (Minor League Baseball) level, and where players are most likely to be called up to the parent Major League team. Players in this league make a salary of about $15,000.

Tennessee Smokies

Double A

The Double-A classification usually hosts developing players that have been part of professional baseball for only a couple of years. These players can get to the Double-A level by earning a promotion from any of the lower-level leagues. Players in Double-A make a salary of around $9,350.
